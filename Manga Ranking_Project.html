All Time Best Manga's accoridng to Vivek

My top 3 Favourite mangas of all-time

  • Tales of Demon and Gods

    • Nie Li, the strongest Demon Spiritualist, is killed by the Sage Emperor and six deity-ranked beasts. Instead of dying, his soul is sent back in time to his 13-year-old self. He wakes up in a classroom, back when he was the weakest student with the most "pathetic" talent.

  • Regressors Tale of Cultivation

    • Seo Eun-hyun is a man with absolutely zero talent for cultivation. While others become immortal geniuses in a few years, he struggles for decades just to reach the first stage. However, every time he dies, he regresses (restarts his life) from the beginning.

  • Eternally Regressing Knights

    • A loyal, skilled knight is betrayed and killed at the end of a catastrophic war. Instead of staying dead, he is caught in a loop of infinite regression. He wakes up as his younger self, forced to live the same life over and over, trying to prevent the destruction of his kingdom and the death of those he sworn to protect.
